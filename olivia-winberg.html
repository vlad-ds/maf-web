Biography

Olivia Winberg is a Master's student at Sciences Po Paris (PSIA) and Columbia University (SIPA) specializing in international economic policy with a focus on financial stability.

Her academic interests center on financial governance, market dynamics, and their interdependencies in shaping global policy outcomes. She brings a comparative perspective to understanding how different institutional frameworks approach financial regulation and stability.

As an organizing team member of the Money as Finance Research Network, Winberg contributes to coordinating research activities and managing network communications, while pursuing her studies in international economic policy at two leading institutions.

Research Interests

Financial Stability

Specialized focus on understanding financial stability mechanisms and their role in international economic policy.

Financial Governance

Research interests in how financial governance structures shape market outcomes and policy effectiveness.

Market Dynamics

Examining the interdependencies between market dynamics and global policy outcomes in financial systems.
