Biography

John Haskell is a dedicated member of the Money as Finance Research Network organizing team. He brings expertise in financial research and academic coordination to support the network's mission.

Haskell's background spans financial theory and practical applications in monetary economics. He contributes to research dissemination and helps coordinate collaborative projects within the network community.

His work involves facilitating communication between researchers, organizing academic events, and supporting the development of innovative research initiatives in the field of money and finance.

Research Interests

Monetary Economics

Research focus on contemporary monetary theory and its practical applications.

Academic Coordination

Facilitating research collaboration and communication within the academic community.

Research Dissemination

Supporting the publication and distribution of cutting-edge research in money and finance.
