About the Workshop Series

The "Money as if Finance Mattered" workshop series focuses on developing a credit money approach that moves away from representative agent models toward modeling a liquidity-based hierarchy of dynamic, time-indexed balance sheets.

Our workshops address the gap between monetary models that often abstract from finance and financial models that tend to ignore the temporal and structural role of money.
