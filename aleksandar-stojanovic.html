Biography

Aleksandar Stojanovic is a key member of the Money as Finance Research Network organizing team. His work focuses on coordinating research activities and facilitating collaboration within the network.

With expertise in financial economics and monetary theory, Stojanovic contributes to the network's mission of advancing understanding of money as finance relationships in contemporary economic systems.

He plays an essential role in organizing conferences, workshops, and research initiatives that bring together scholars from diverse backgrounds to explore innovative approaches to monetary and financial analysis.

Research Interests

Financial Economics

Focus on the intersection of monetary theory and financial market dynamics.

Network Coordination

Organizing research collaborations and facilitating academic exchanges within the network.

Conference Organization

Leading efforts to organize academic conferences and workshops on money and finance topics.
